person About Dr. Jenkins

Dr. Sarah Jenkins is a highly distinguished Senior Cardiologist with over 15 years of experience in cardiovascular medicine. She specializes in interventional cardiology and has performed thousands of successful procedures at The London Clinic.

Areas of Expertise

  • Complex Coronary Interventions
  • Structural Heart Disease Management
  • Hypertension & Preventive Cardiology
  • Advanced Cardiac Imaging (Echo & CT)

A graduate of Oxford University Medical School, she completed her fellowship at the Royal Brompton Hospital. She is a fellow of the Royal College of Physicians (FRCP) and active contributor to several international medical journals.
